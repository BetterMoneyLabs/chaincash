## The Beginning: Bitcoin in 2009

### Satoshi's Vision

In early 2009, Satoshi released the source code along with the whitepaper titled:

> **"A Peer-to-Peer Electronic Cash System"**

---

## Not the First Attempt

### Early P2P Cash Systems

- **DigiCash (1989)** - David Chaum's blind signature-based electronic cash w. trusted issuance
- **Mojo Nation Money (2001)** - distributed virtual currency of a p2p filesharing service
- **iWat (2003)** - *"Peer-to-peer money: free currency over the internet"*

---

## What Made Bitcoin Different?

### The Missing Pieces

- **Proof-of-Work consensus** - no central authority needed
- **Blockchain** - immutable public ledger
- **Trustless issuance** - via an algorithm 

---

## But does it work as p2p cash?

### Since 2010 community realized it is not

- Blocks are slow and limited
- Bitcoin asset is volatile due to issuance properties

---

## Different experimentation

- Lightning (offchain payments backed 1:1 by on-chain reserves)
- Proof-of-Stake / BFT blockchains 
- Algorithmic and then centralized stablecoins
